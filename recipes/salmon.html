Spicy Salmon

Description

Try to create this dank salmon recipe for your guilty pleasures!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Chipotle Chili Powder
  • Lime
  • Brown Sugar

Instructions

  1. Let the salmon sit at room temperature for 10 minutes, then pat it dry. Place it on a piece of aluminum foil, set atop a large rimmed baking pan (if your foil isnâ€™t large enough, layer to pieces together).
  2. Stir the spice rub ingredients together. Brush the salmon with oil and lime juice, then coat it in the spice rub.
  3. Fold the foil over the salmon and seal it. Bake salmon at 375 degrees F for 12 to 18 minutes. Open the foil. Broil for 3 minutes. The internal temperature should be around 135 degrees F.
  4. Cut into salmon fillets, serve with cilantro, and DIG IN!
