Eggs Benedict

Description

Learn how to cook a bomb eggs benedict for when you're hungover af

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons rice vinegar
  • 2 English muffins
  • Butter
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Hollandaise Sauce

  • 10 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Dash cayenne or Tabasco sauce
